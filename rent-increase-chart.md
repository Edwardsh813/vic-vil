## Why Incremental is Better

1. **Lower turnover** - Big jumps (35%+) cause move-outs; 10-12% annual increases are more digestible
2. **Market testing** - You'll see how tenants respond before committing to premium pricing
3. **Gradual market positioning** - Gives you time to upgrade units to justify higher rents
4. **Less vacancy loss** - Turnover costs ~1-2 months rent per unit; avoiding it pays for itself

## Risk of Going Straight to Target

- 35% increase could trigger significant move-outs
- Target 2BR ($900) is above current market - may struggle to fill vacancies
- Nelsonville tenant base may not support premium pricing yet
